Accessibility and equity in emergency services is complex. To mitigate this problem, out team partnered with accesSOS, a nonprofit organization that aims to make emergency services available to people who cannot call 911.

We set out to learn about the emergency services domain, understand how diverse populations interact with the services, and how we can develop a trusted solution that meets their needs.
